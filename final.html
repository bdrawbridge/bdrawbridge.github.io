Welcome to my Final Project

Welcome to my Final Project which is a spinoff of the classic arcade game, Galaga. In this game you move back and fourth using the A & D keys and hit as many aliens as you can to gain points. You gain 1 point for every alien you kill, but for every alien that gets passed you lose 2 points. How high of a score can you get?
